About Krisik Bazar

Empowering Nepali farmers through technology

Krisik Bazar is a platform designed to help Nepali farmers get the best prices for their crops. Our mission is to empower farmers with transparent market information and reduce the exploitation by middlemen.

By providing real-time price information and market locations, we help farmers make informed decisions about where to sell their products for maximum profit.

Our Vision

To create a fair and transparent agricultural marketplace that benefits both farmers and buyers across Nepal.
